CPU scheduling code

December, 2019

  • First Come First Serve (FCFS): This algorithm schedules processes based on their arrival time, executing the process that arrived first before moving on to the next one.
  • Round Robin (RR): This algorithm allocates a fixed time slice for each process, and after the time slice is up, the next process in the queue is executed.
  • Shortest Job First (SJF): This algorithm schedules processes based on their burst time, executing the process with the shortest burst time first.
  • Shortest Remaining Time First (SRTF or SJRF): This algorithm schedules processes based on their remaining burst time, executing the process with the shortest remaining burst time first.

Through my knowledge of Python programming, I have successfully designed and implemented four CPU scheduling codes.
